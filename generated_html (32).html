Lava Agni 2 5G Summary
Lava Agni 2 5G mobile was launched on 16th May 2023. The phone comes with a 120 Hz refresh rate 6.78-inch touchscreen display offering a resolution of 2220x2080 pixels (FHD+). Lava Agni 2 5G is powered by an octa-core MediaTek Dimensity 7050 processor. It comes with 8GB of RAM. The Lava Agni 2 5G runs Android 13 and is powered by a 4700mAh non-removable battery. The Lava Agni 2 5G supports 66W Fast Charging fast charging.
